# v0.1.116 — Edge Drawer & Production Speed

Major UI refactor: the old building panel is replaced by a unified drawer system. At the same time, production speed becomes adjustable per building.

## New
- **Edge drawer system**: Buildings, Transport and Tasks now share a common drawer architecture docked to the screen edge
- **Build picker**, **building list** and **recipe picker** as separate views inside the drawer
- **Tile system** for unified display of buildings, items, routes and upgrades
- **Production speed editor**: a gear button on the building opens a slider to throttle the target utilization (target_efficiency)
- Bidirectional sync between the slider and the rate fields; rate labels use the throttled effective efficiency
- Live updating of the rate labels on efficiency changes

## Improved
- `building_detail_panel.gd` (2495 lines) replaced by modular sections/views in `src/ui/edge_drawer/`
- `transport_drawer.gd` and `task_dialog.gd` significantly slimmed down (shared `drawer_content_base`)
- The build picker updates its tiles live on inventory changes while the menu is open
- Editors are cancelled consistently on drawer close, building switch or navigation
