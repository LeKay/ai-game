# v0.1.130 — Logistics Reservations & Map Polish

Stabilization of the logistics and reservation system, plus further refinements to the drag controller and the save manager.

## New
- Reservation bookkeeping in `InventoryContainer` and `InventorySystem`
- New tests:
  - `reservation_lockup_test` (integration) — prevents deadlocks with multiple carriers
  - `input_reservation_test` (unit)
  - `reservation_test` (unit)

## Improved
- `LogisticsSystem`: stronger guarantees in the reservation lifecycle
- `map_root` drag controller, `resource_badge_layer` and `building_indicator_layer` polished
- `WorldGrid`, `OverworldSystem`, `PlayerCharacter` and `CameraController` with minor corrections
- `WorldSaveManager` saves/loads the new state correctly

## Fixes
- Carrier waiting test adjusted to the new reservation behavior
